Eron Harris

Eron Harris, from Indianapolis, played his first two seasons at West Virginia University, where he was one of the team’s leading scorers. He averaged 17.2 points per game as a sophomore. He sat out last season because of NCAA transfer rules.

Harris has shown flashes of why fans were so excited to get him on the team with his unique ability to knock down the three and also drive to the hoop at will to show off his incredible athleticism.

Harris has become a huge part of MSU’s season, even more since Lourawls “Tum Tum” Nairn Jr. went down with plantar fasciitis. Harris is currently averaging 9.3 points per game.
