Alvin Ellis III

Ellis, from Matteson, Ill. was teammates with Gavin Schilling at De La Salle Institute in Chicago during the 2011-12 season.

Ellis has shown glimpses of the skill Izzo saw in him when he was originally being recruited, including a career-high 16-point effort in a game against Indiana during the 2014-15 season and providing a spark off the bench on Feb. 9 against Purdue, scoring six points and one block.
